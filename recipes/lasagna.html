Lasagna

Description

Lasagne are a type os pasta, possubly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. The term refers to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • cloves,garlic(crushed)
  • Can crushed tomato paste
  • White Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground bef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crused tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and wayer. Season with Brown sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1teaspoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lenghtwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixutre. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmasen cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make the foil does not touch the cheese.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
